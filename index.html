About DPmaps

Version: 1.0.0 • Leaflet version:

  • Save/Load drawn features in .geojson format.
  • Export drawn features in Excel.
  • Hover over (desktop) or touch (mobile) Cadastre to view property address.

Tip: Right-click on a cadastre parcel (desktop) or long-press (mobile) to launch Google Street View.

Limitations

  • Drawn features are not automatically saved and will be lost on page refresh.
  • Address search uses Leaflet/Nominatim and may not return house address.
